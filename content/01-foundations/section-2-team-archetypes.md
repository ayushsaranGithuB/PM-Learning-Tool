# Section 2 — Team Archetypes

## Learning Goal

Recognize the different types of product organizations and understand where Product Managers create the most impact.

## Reading

### The Good, the Bad and the Ugly

https://timwoods.io/category/marty-cagan/

Search for:

"The Good, the Bad, and the Ugly"

Learn the difference between:

- Delivery Teams
- Feature Teams
- Empowered Product Teams

---

### The Product Triad

https://www.nngroup.com/articles/the-product-triad-designs-role/

Focus on how Product, Design and Engineering share ownership of outcomes.

---

## Key Takeaways

- Delivery Teams build what they're told.
- Feature Teams optimize for output.
- Empowered Product Teams solve customer problems.
- Great products come from strong collaboration between Product, Design and Engineering.
