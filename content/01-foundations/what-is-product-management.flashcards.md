# Flashcards: What Is Product Management?

## Cards

### `what-is-pm-01`

Prompt: A team ships features every sprint, but retention does not improve. What product management question should come before brainstorming more features?

Answer: The team should first clarify what user problem or behavior needs to change and how success will be measured.

### `what-is-pm-02`

Prompt: Why is "product management is decision management" a stronger mental model than "product management is feature management"?

Answer: Because the PM's real job is to help the team make better decisions about problems, priorities, trade-offs, and outcomes rather than simply producing more features.

### `what-is-pm-03`

Prompt: A designer, engineer, and PM disagree about what to build next. What is the PM uniquely responsible for contributing?

Answer: The PM should bring clarity on the user problem, the business context, the trade-offs, and the definition of success so the team can make a better decision together.
