Metrics and Growth

Sources: YC Analytics for Startups, YC Growth Strategy.

Learning Goal: Understand product through measurement. Founders often track dozens of metrics, but great PMs track very few. YC emphasizes focusing on leading indicators. Instead of revenue, look at activation. Instead of downloads, look at retention. Instead of page views, look at weekly active users. Metrics should explain behavior, not decorate dashboards.

Growth is not marketing; growth is removing friction. Every funnel asks: Where are users leaving? Why? What assumption failed? Good PMs think experimentally: Hypothesis → Experiment → Measurement → Learning → Next experiment.

Takeaways: Measure behavior, measure habits, measure value delivered.
Vanity metrics are distractions.

Exercise: Take Spotify:

- Map its onboarding funnel
- Identify where users may drop off
- Suggest one measurable experiment.
